WHAT IS DORM TROOPERS?

A website designed for university students primarily living in dorms provided by a university. It is activvated by an individual user IDs of a particular university and helps them navigaate through the college campus. Encompassing of all its college information with cafes, libraries, study rooms, etc,. The website becomes an easy guide for a fresher. The student body president can update all kinds of news & announcements here for the students to keep them up to date. As a special feature, the website also allows to host parties & other various activites that can be supervised. Other than all these features, it also gives the students access to set reminders (eeg. Laundry, Study, etc,.) and let's them track their progress hassle-free.
